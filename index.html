About

Gentle Robot is an indie rock band from Chapel Hill, NC. It began as Tim Sookram's solo project in 2007. In 2012, he began collaborating with drummer Tim Wall. Gentle Robot made their debut on June 1st, 2012 at Deep South Bar in Raleigh. Since then, the group has had a number of well-received performances in the Triangle area, including Local 506 and the Carrboro Music Festival.
